## Capacity Guardrails

- Commitments are framed as capacity, roles, cadence, and operating model unless
  source material explicitly defines fixed deliverables.
- Backlog priority, acceptance, and tradeoff decisions must have a named client
  owner.
- Unused capacity, overflow demand, after-hours work, specialist roles, or new
  fixed deliverables require explicit handling in the SOW or change process.
- Rates, fees, and payment terms remain in approved external commercial
  artifacts unless explicitly supplied for final SOW text.
