## Outcome Guardrails

- Define each outcome with measurable evidence and a responsible owner.
- Separate AIS-controlled delivery from client-controlled adoption,
  stakeholder, data, and operating dependencies.
- If target outcomes depend on assumptions that change, route the adjustment
  through change control or the agreed commercial review path.
- Do not imply pricing, credits, holdbacks, or incentives unless supplied in an
  approved commercial artifact.
