milestones, or an approved fixed fee. This stub shapes the scope and acceptance
model; it is not a pricing worksheet.

## FFP Guardrails

- AIS commits to the named deliverables and source-stated acceptance criteria.
- Client delays, missing access, material scope changes, new compliance
  requirements, or expanded acceptance criteria may require a change order.
- Pricing, payment terms, rates, and profitability stay in approved external
  commercial artifacts unless the user explicitly provides final SOW values.
